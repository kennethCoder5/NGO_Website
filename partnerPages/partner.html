Innovation Learnex Foundation is one of the biggest non-governmental development organisation in the world, measured by innovation, impact and governance.

Innvasia Learnex Foundation came into being in 2022 with its flagship programme Mission Education and has since been working with the objective of empowering underprivileged children by providing education, nutrition, and healthcare support. The Mission Education programme caters to Preschool (3-6 years old), Formal Education (6-18 years school going children),

Innvasia Learnex Foundation started its health programme with a view to provide healthcare to all irrespective of location, with a special focus on urban poor, villagers Keeping the health of children, women mobile health clinics were started which could reach out to people in remote areas or informal settlements of the cities where people lack awareness about basic healthcare.

Innvasia Learnex Foundation realised that with a little hand-holding, youth can become the backbone of the Indian economy. If they are shown the right direction, their family prospers and so does the country. The aim was and is to help as many youth as possible in getting gainful employment. They include those who have dropped out of school and those who have completed their Class X and XII.
